# Trade admission authority

Sensei separates intelligence from capital authority.

- The scheduler/Desk Head coordinates work but cannot approve an order.
- Historian, Reporter, Crowd Reader, Analyst, Devil's Advocate, Compliance and
  Coach outputs are evidence. They may be produced outside the entry window.
- The entry path makes no LLM call. Registered policy checks risk, thesis
  coherence, public provenance, surveillance and regime evidence.
- The existing L1-L4 labels remain in the journal for schema compatibility;
  they are deterministic check groups, not conversational approval rounds.
- `TradeCommitteeApproved` is the single aggregated decision for the exact
  intent. `PaperIntentAdmissionAuthorized` is the signed capability accepted by
  the execution kernel.
- A failed required check vetoes. Advisory opinions never outvote a safety
  failure, and the Desk Head cannot override either result.

Slow agents belong in research, pre-market preparation and post-trade review.
Their artifacts must be signed, version-bound and fresh before deterministic
policy may use them. Missing advisory evidence is reported; missing required
safety evidence fails closed.
